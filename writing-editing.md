# Writing Editing

## Banned Words: Before publishing, search for the following words and remove them when possible. 

1. Just
1. That
1. Already
1. Actual / Actually
1. Think
1. Pretty
1. Really
1. To be
1. Great
1. Got
1. Around
1. A lot
1. Very
1. Thing
1. Much
1. Unfortunate / fortunate
1. Nice
1. More

## Banned Phrasings

1. I think
1. I believe
1. It seems
